Alignments used in study for all analyses.

trim0_ : refers to the portion of gaps trimmed; trim01 = 10% trimmed, trim03 = 30% trimmed, trim05 = 50% trimmed, trim07 = 70% trimmed

1) concatenated: alignments of concatenated B and B' sequences usef for Fig 1, FigS6 trees
2) paralog: alignements of B to B' sequences used for Fig 5, Fig S8
3) lba_assess: alignments used in the Long Branch Attraction assessments
4) MCP_TerL: alignments of major capsid protein (MCP) sequences and terminase large subunit (TerL) sequences used in Fig 4.
